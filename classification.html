Film Classifications

All film classification decisions are based on the British Board of Film Classification's (BBFC) published and regularly updated guidelines.

For more detailed information about BBFC rulings, and for information for parents regarding film classifications and their suitability, please visit www.bbfc.co.uk (opens in a new window)
